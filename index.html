1. Introduction

We build free and paid mobile games distributed via Google Play and other app stores. We value your privacy and aim to be transparent about the data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. This policy describes how we collect, store, use, share, and protect information obtained through our mobile applications (the “Apps” or “Service”).

By installing or using our Apps you consent to the collection and processing described in this policy. If you are under the age of 13 (or under 16 where applicable), please review the section Children’s Privacy below.

2. Data We Collect

A. Information You Provide

Some features (optional) may ask you to supply personal information:

  • Name or nickname (for leaderboards)
  • Email address (for support or account recovery)
  • Support messages, feedback, or bug reports

B. Automatically Collected Information

When you use our Apps, third-party SDKs may automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version, advertising ID, language)
  • Usage data (session time, levels played, features used)

C. Advertising & Analytics Identifiers

We and our partners use advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID) for analytics and monetization. These identifiers do not personally identify you.

We use collected data to:

  • Show ads and measure ad performance

Our apps may display ads from networks such as AdMob. Depending on your region and consent, ads may be personalized or non-personalized. You can manage ad personalization via your device settings or Google Ads Settings.

5. Third-Party SDKs & Services

We use trusted third-party services for analytics, ads, and crash reporting, including:

  • Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics)

Each provider has its own privacy policy. Please review their terms to understand their data practices.

6. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or 16 where applicable). For child-directed experiences, we only use limited data collection and non-personalized ads.

If you believe your child has provided personal information, contact us at gamedevlopment.freelancing@gmail.com for prompt removal.
